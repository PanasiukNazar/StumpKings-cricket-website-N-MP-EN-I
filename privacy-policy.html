Welcome to our Cookie Policy. This policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies in connection with Fantasy Crickets tournaments.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help enhance your experience and improve Fantasy Crickets tournament services.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes, including:

  • Optimizing the performance of Fantasy Crickets tournament features
  • Remembering your preferences and settings for Fantasy Crickets
  • Enhancing user experience by personalizing Fantasy Crickets content
  • Analyzing website traffic to improve Fantasy Crickets tournaments
  • Providing relevant updates and notifications about Fantasy Crickets tournaments

4. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core Fantasy Crickets tournament functionalities.

  • Functional Cookies: Store user preferences related to Fantasy Crickets tournaments.
  • Analytics Cookies: Gather insights to enhance Fantasy Crickets platform performance.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain Fantasy Crickets tournament features.
