Master Fantasy Cricket Tournaments

Fantasy crickets offer an exciting way to engage with the game, test your cricketing knowledge, and compete with others. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, understanding the key steps is essential for success.

Build your team, strategize wisely, and maximize your points to win big. Join fantasy cricket tournaments and experience the thrill of managing your dream team in real-time matches.

Step 1: Select a Tournament

Choose from a variety of fantasy cricket tournaments based on real matches. Different formats allow you to pick daily or season-long contests, offering flexibility in gameplay.

Step 2: Create Your Team

Build your fantasy cricket team by selecting players within the given budget. Pick a balanced squad of batsmen, bowlers, all-rounders, and a wicketkeeper to increase your chances of winning.

Step 3: Choose Captain & Vice-Captain

Selecting the right captain and vice-captain is crucial. The captain earns double points, while the vice-captain gets 1.5x points. Smart choices can significantly boost your ranking.

Step 4: Track Live Scores & Rankings

Follow live matches to see how your players perform. Your fantasy cricket team's score updates in real time based on their on-field performance, keeping the excitement alive.
